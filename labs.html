MathML

Einstein's Theory of Relativity Equation

E = m c 2

This was created with MathML, which was based on XML. MathML is better for non-Web based applications, and was originally supported only on FireFox with a very small proportion of supported answers. Only recently has the language become supported on all modern browsers, with some serious limitations to this day.

Einstein's special theory of relativity states that the same laws of physics hold true in all inertial reference frames and that the speed of light is the same for all observers, even those moving with respect to one another.
